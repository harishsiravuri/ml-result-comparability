## Evidence text is not redistributed
We do not include excerpt text from the source papers. Each record points to its source (arXiv
identifier, URL, and section or table location) and the reported value. To read the exact evidence,
consult the cited arXiv paper directly. This keeps the release free of third-party paper text.
